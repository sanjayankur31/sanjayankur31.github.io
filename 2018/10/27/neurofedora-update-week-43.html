NeuroFedora update: week 43

Posted by ankur in Research (130 words, approximately a 1 minute read)

  • #Community, #Computational neuroscience, #Free software, #NEST, #Fedora, #NeuroFedora

  • Share this post:

NeuroFedora logo by Terezahl from the Fedora Design Team

In week 43:

  • libneurosim was reviewed and approved and is currently in testing. PyNN needs NEST to be built with libneurosim support, so that'll have to be done before PyNN can be submitted for review. That's next on the list.
  • libNeuroML was also reviewed and approved this week, and is now in testing. It should be available for use in the stable repositories in a week. I can now work on packaging up pyNeuroML.

There is a lot of software available in NeuroFedora already. You can see the list here. If you use software that is not on our list, please suggest it to us using the suggestion form.

Feedback is always welcome. You can get in touch with us here.
